Submission guidelines

Rules & eligibility

  • Open to all current high school students 18 years of age or younger.
  • Films must be 15 minutes or under in length, including credits.
  • Films must fall into one of three categories: Narrative, Animation, or Documentary.
  • Films must be original work by the submitting student or student group.
  • Accepted formats: MP4, MOV, or AVI (HD 1080p preferred).
  • Each student or team may submit up to one film.
  • Submissions are free of charge.
  • Selected films will be screened at the Bank Street Theater before a live audience and judged by a panel of arts and film professionals.
